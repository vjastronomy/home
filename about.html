About Us

The Victoria Junior College Astronomy Club is a co-curricular activity (CCA) club in Victoria Junior College (VJC) comprised of individuals passionate about astronomy. The club is dedicated to the study and appreciation of celestial objects and phenomena, across domains of theoretical and practical astronomy.

What is the typical club session like?

We typically meet on Friday evenings (around 6 pm) at designated classrooms.

Each session is usually divided into two periods:

  • a "Theory" period (where we discuss and learn about some of the more theoretical aspects of astronomy), followed by
  • an "Observation" period (where we get to stargaze and make observations, with the help of various equipment such as telescopes) that extends till late (around 9 pm).
